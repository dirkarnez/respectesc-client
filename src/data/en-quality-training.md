# Quality and training of overseas domestic helpers
**Selection done by dedicated personnel** 
Our domestic helpers from Philippines, Cambodia, Indonesia are interviewed and selected by the stationed personnels. Personnels in each area have a recruitment code, they also regularly carry out large-scale recruitment activities in various regions. By staying closer to the domestic helper applicants’ family background, we can know their origin and personal character better for the preliminary selection. After that, they will be evaluated based on their appearance, academic qualifications, work experience and other aspects. During the interview, the applicant's performance and attitude are also important for admission. Successful applicants will be arranged to take photos and make self-introduction as their profile for employers to choose from.

**Overseas Training Centres**  
we requires that some domestic helpers must complete domestic helper training in overseas training centres before arriving in Hong Kong to ensure that domestic helpers receive comprehensive training.

**Health condition of overseas domestic helper**  
Before arriving in Hong Kong, overseas domestic helper will undergo a detailed body check. They can then leave the country if they are in good health. The selection, training, body check, arrangements and follow-ups are done by dedicated personnel to ensure that employers are satisfied.
