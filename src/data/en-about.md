**Respect Employment Services Centre (Chinese: 護家僱傭服務中心)** is the best domestic helper employment agency in Hong Kong. The Centre cooperates closely with various regions in Southeast Asia, recommends especially Filipino, Cambodian and Indonesian domestic helpers, and regularly sends staff to the local training centre for rigorous selection and training to ensure that every domestic helper is in best quality.

The Centre serves you wholeheartedly. We firmly believe that honesty and high transparency are important for our long-term relationship. We are committed to build long-term relationship with employers, to make them happy to enjoy our services again. With the professional knowledge and experience accumulated over the years, we are confident to find the most suitable domestic helper for you.

**Comprehensive domestic helper follow-up service**

**Our relationship with customers is not limited to one-time service.**

We carefully follow up every customer application, ensure that the domestic helper arrives in Hong Kong and customers' residence on time. For the domestic helpers who still need guidance after they start working, we have specialists who provide Filipino, Cambodian and Indonesian translations and are responsible for teaching, to help them adapt to the Hong Kong family environment as soon as possible and quickly master the skills for housework. We guarantee high quality services and pleasant experience.

**Our mission:**  

> To offer the best-quality domestic helpers to employers, thereby alleviating employers' stress and promoting harmonious employment relationships.
