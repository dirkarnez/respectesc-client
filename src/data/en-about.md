# Company Profile
**Respect Employment Services Centre (Chinese: 護家僱傭服務中心)** provides the best foreign domestic helper intermediary services in the foreign domestic helper industry in Hong Kong. The Centre cooperates closely with various regions in Southeast Asia, recommends especially Filipino, Cambodian and Indian domestic helpers, and regularly sends staff to the local training Centre for rigorous selection and training to ensure that each is the best quality maid.

We serve you wholeheartedly, we firmly believe that honesty and high transparency are important for our long-term relationship. We are committed to establishing long-term relationship with employers, so that customers are happy to cooperate with us again. With the professional knowledge and experience accumulated in the past, we are confident to find the most suitable maid for you.

**Comprehensive follow-up maid service**

**Our relationship with customers is not limited to one-time service. **

We carefully follow up every guest's application to ensure that the domestic helper arrives in Hong Kong on time and that the domestic helper arrives at the guest's home on time. For domestic helpers who still need guidance after they are integrated into the home, we have specialists who provide Filipino, Cambodian and Indonesian translations and are responsible for teaching so that the helpers can adapt to the Hong Kong family environment as soon as possible and quickly master the operation of housework. Please rest assured, use it happily.

**Our service tenet: Recommend the best quality maids to employers, reduce employers' pressure, and promote harmony between employers and employers. **
